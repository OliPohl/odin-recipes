Der Patrick

Patrick’s hunger for cheddar had finally crossed the line into a full-blown obsession. He sat amidst a chaotic sea of rinds, frantically shoveling huge wedges of brie and gorgonzola into his mouth as if he couldn't stop. By the time the table was picked clean, Patrick felt less like a man and more like a heavy, groaning wheel of dairy.

Ingredients

  • 2kg Cheese
  • 1kg Chicken breast
  • 1kg diffrent Cheese

Preparation

  1. Put the cheese on a baking tray
  2. Put the chicken breast in the cheese
  3. Top it of with cheese
  4. Bake for 30 minutes
