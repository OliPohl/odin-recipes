French Breakfast

A classic french breakfast is the perfect start into the day. Its healty, easy to make and gives a lot of energy. Thats why over 60 milion french people choose it as their goto breakfast.

It consists of a very strong coffee and a pack of cigarettes (sometimes two).

Ingredients

  • Coffee
  • A pack of cigarettes

Preparation

  1. Go to you favorite coffeeshop
  2. Order the cheapest coffee on the menu
  3. Light up a cigarette
  4. Sit there until lunch and look direcly at people walking by
